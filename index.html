Jollof rice

A classic West African one-pot dish made with rice,tomatoes,peppers,and spices.

Recipe detail

Prep time:15 minutes

Cook time:45 minutes

Servings:4

Difficulty:Easy

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomato paste
  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Vegetable oil
  • Pepper mix
  • Seasoning cube

Instruction

  1. Blend the tomatoes,peppers,and onions
  2. Heat oil in a pot and fry tomatoes paste and blended mix
  3. Add seasoning,salt,and bay leaves and let it simmer
  4. Pour in the rice and waterto cook
  5. Cover and cook on low heat until the rice is soft
  6. Stir gently and serve
